The different businesses :

There are three main types of businesses. Each type of business has advantages and disadvantages, allowing you to choose the solution best suited to your visitor situation.

  • Integrated commerce
    • Integrated commerce refers to forms of networked commerce in which all points belong to the same owner.
  • Organized independent trade
    • Organized independent commerce is a form of commerce in which the merchant retains his legal independence and a more or less strong management autonomy while associating with a parent structure (franchisor) or with other merchants.
  • Isolated independent business
    • This type of point of sale is owned by an independent merchant who is not affiliated with any network.
